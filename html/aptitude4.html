Various dependencies that can be satisfied by multiple packages are shown.

aptitude-doc is a virtual package that is provided by various (unspecified) other packages.

libwww-perl has two dependencies with alternatives which have special indicators. The square brackets ("[...]") for libmime-base64-perl and libnet-perl indicate that those packages are also shown separately elsewhere in the graph (and their dependencies will be shown from that separate instance). The braces ("{...}") for perl indicate that its dependencies are not shown in the graph.
